ABOUT ME

Sworn translator (English-Spanish) and lawyer graduated from the University of Buenos Aires.

Graduated in 2013, I have been working ever since as a freelance translator providing services for local clients and foreign agencies.

In 2016, I graduated as a lawyer and worked within the business division of the Laprida and Goñi law firm. There, I was in charge of keeping up to date the corporate documents of local and multinational corporations.

Due to my hybrid background, nowadays I balance my schedule in between translations and court hearings.
